AWARENESS

Consult The Doctor

Whenever you feel fever or body pains for around 2-3 days, do consult a doctor as soon as possible.

No to hand shakes

Do not shake hands with anyone, it may cause spread of the virus. A great way to greet people without direct contact would be to use namaste, finger-guns or imaginary-hat tipping!.

Use sanitizer

Wash your hands with soap and water for at least 20 seconds or use a 60% alcohol-based hand sanitizer.

Stay home stay safe

Stay isolated if you are sick. If you develop COVID-19 symptoms at work, notify your supervisor and go home immediately.

Wear Mask

Always cover your mouth with a mask, it will stop the spread of the covid-19 virus very effectively.

Maintain Social Distancing

Avoid close contact by staying 6 feet apart to reduce risk of exposure and avoid close contact with people who are at risk.

Get vaccinated

Please get vaccinated as soon as possible, it will prevent you to get infected by covid-19 virus. It will help the whole country to get better.

No to Social gatherings

Please do not encourage the gathering of people at a very single place. Gathering increase the spread of the covid-19 virus and may lead to serious conditions.

Clean and Disinfect

Frequently touched surfaces and objects at least daily such as doorknobs, light switches, phones, and keyboards.
